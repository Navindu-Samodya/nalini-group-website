Why Choose Solar Power?

Discover the real-world benefits of switching to clean, renewable energy

Cut Your Electricity Bills

Solar panels generate free electricity from sunlight. Most households reduce their electricity bills by 60–80% within the first year of installation. Over a 25-year lifespan, the savings easily outweigh the upfront cost.

Zero Emissions, Zero Guilt

Solar power produces no greenhouse gases during operation. By switching from a diesel generator to solar, the average Sri Lankan home prevents over 2 tonnes of CO₂ from entering the atmosphere every year.

Energy Independence

Say goodbye to CEB outages and rising electricity bills. With the right solar + battery system, your home or business gets reliable 24/7 power regardless of the national grid.

Low Maintenance

Solar panels have no moving parts, meaning virtually zero mechanical failures. A quick clean every few months is all that is needed. Most systems run trouble-free for 25+ years with minimal intervention.

Increases Property Value

Properties with solar installations consistently sell for more and attract buyers faster. Solar is now considered a premium feature that signals forward-thinking ownership and reduced running costs.

Sri Lanka's Natural Advantage

Sri Lanka receives an average of 5–7 peak sun hours per day — among the highest in the world. This makes solar panels here significantly more productive than in Europe or North America.

25-Year Panel Warranty

Our solar panels come with a 25-year performance guarantee and a 10-year product warranty. You can invest with full confidence in long-term, reliable energy production.

Hedge Against Rising Tariffs

Electricity tariffs in Sri Lanka have risen dramatically year on year. Once your solar system is installed, your cost per unit of energy is locked in close to zero — forever.

Battery Storage Included

Our systems pair seamlessly with lithium battery banks to store energy generated during the day for use at night. No sunshine? No problem — your batteries keep everything running smoothly.

How Solar Works

From sunlight to socket — a simple 4-step journey

1

Sunlight Hits Panels

Photovoltaic (PV) cells in the solar panels absorb sunlight and convert it into direct current (DC) electricity.

2

Inverter Converts Power

The solar inverter converts DC electricity into alternating current (AC), the standard power your appliances use.

3

Power Your Home

AC electricity flows through your home's electrical system, powering lights, fans, TVs, fridges — everything.

4

Store the Surplus

Excess energy is stored in lithium battery banks, keeping your home powered after sunset or on cloudy days.
